West to the sea

Direction :
  • Morgan Cardiff
Release year : 2015
Production :
  • Rhys Morgan Images
Country : Canada
Language : English
Starring :
  • Dave Chamberlain

In 2013 Dave Chamberlain did the impossible, he became the first person to run, unsupported, across Canada. Running from Cape Spear, Newfoundland to Toffino, BC. he ran 8100 km in a remarkable 5 months and 1 day. West to the Sea examines the driving force behind this project, the ideas of possibility and what people believe to be possible.
